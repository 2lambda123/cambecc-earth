SUMMARY

Nature has an almost magical way to create oases in the midst of extremely harsh environments, and to create microclimates where the temperature can fall more than 35 degrees Celsius by moving less than a kilometer. What if we study the way nature creates those oases and microclimates, optimize it, and use it to create a comfortable, controlled outdoors experience? The value is tremendous, affecting everything from lifestyle and culture to tourism and business. This is the technology that Rayyan has developed in stealth mode over the last five years. As the pioneering company in creating smart microclimates, Rayyan has created the technology that helps create a dynamic outdoors oasis in an area up to 10 squared kilometers. This oasis can be 25 degrees Celsius cooler than the area surrounding it, and will have moderate humidity and breeze. With global warming effects looming, a green, sustainable, and scalable solution for outdoor climate control is starting to be an important part of urban and city planning. Rayyan’s leading architecture and technology puts it at the pivot of leading this change in how we design our living environments. Rayyan’s technology is best integrated in the early stages of an urban project, but can be integrated later. It is 93% more efficient than “traditional” solutions in indoors areas, and is the only sustainable outdoors microclimate solution.

WHY US

Rayyan has a patented technology and architecture that is the result of years of research in microclimate, green architecture, and distributed controls systems. We have partnered with leading technology, energy, and construction firms to ensure the best execution and results of the project. Our Smart Oasis Architecture allows the creation of an open-air controlled microclimate on an area of up to 10 squared Km. The weather and experience in the oasis includes everything from the cool breeze to the greenery and water that gives a higher quality of life. As the weather, temperature, and wind outside the oasis change, the dynamic control system reacts in real time to keep the optimal experience. All of this is done in the most sustainable and energy efficient manner possible, helping us have a greener world too.

WHERE WE COME IN

There are three places where Rayyan adds value to a development project

1

At the design/early implementation phase: Here Rayyan would create the architecture and the design of the Smart Oasis and integrate it into the plan. This is a collaborative effort with the developer.

2

During the implementation phase, Rayyan offers the construction partners software tools and expert support to ensure the system is implemented within spec. We also bring in our partners who have experience in creating several parts of the system.

3

During the life of the project, Rayyan provides the management, updates, maintenance, and support of the system. This includes running the real time distributed control system to react to the changing weather in the surroundings.

MISSION

Rayyan sustainably creates oases of eternal spring living.
